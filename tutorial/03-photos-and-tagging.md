# Chapter 3: Photos and Tagging

Your logs aren't complete without the incredible sights you captured. Pelagic Desktop features deep integration for managing, viewing, and tagging your underwater photography.

## 1. Importing Photos

Once your dives are set up, you can import images to attach to specific trips or directly to single dives.

1. Click on **Import Photos** in the Global Header.
2. Select files or entire directories containing your underwater photos.
3. The integrated importer allows you to review images and map them to specific dates or existing dive logs automatically.

## 2. The Photo Viewer

To see your imported images in high quality:

1. Navigate to a specific trip or dive.
2. The Content Area will populate with a gallery of available thumbnails.
3. Click any photo to launch the **Photo Viewer** – a dedicated lightbox interface.

From the viewer, you can cycle through all images in a dive entirely, zoom in, and prepare them for detailed metadata updates.

![Photo Viewer Lightbox](./screenshots/photo-viewer.png)

## 3. The Right Panel Metadata Editor

When interacting with single or multiple photos from the Content Grid or within the Photo Viewer, the **Right Panel** appears on the side of the screen.

This powerful inspector allows you to:
- **Edit core file metadata:** Update capture time, adjust location, or modify standard EXIF fields.
- **Manage associations:** Re-assign a photo from one dive to another if the time sync was slightly off.
- **Bulk Edit Attributes:** Select multiple images in the gallery grid and use the right panel to apply the same location or gear used across a batch.

## 4. Tagging (Marine Species and General)

What’s a dive without identifying the marine life? 

Pelagic offers two robust tagging systems:
- **Species Tags:** Use the Species Tag Modal to catalog exact marine life (corals, fish, macro subjects).
- **General Tags:** Employ the General Tag Modal for labeling behavior, environmental conditions, or subjective terms like "Wide-Angle", "Macro", or "Wreck".

To tag a photo:
1. Select the photo in the grid.
2. In the Right Panel, locate the **Tags** section.
3. Begin typing to select from your saved database of species or general tags, or use AI-assisted tools (if configured via API) to suggest labels.
